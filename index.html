Our Mission & Vision

Dedicated to preserving, promoting, and revitalizing Mro cultural heritage through literature, arts, and community engagement.

Preserve Heritage

Documenting and archiving Mro literature, traditions, and cultural practices for future generations.

Build Community

Creating spaces for cultural exchange and strengthening bonds within the Mro community.

Educate & Share

Promoting Mro language, literature, and cultural education across generations.
